About MechAI+

Our Mission

Empowering rural communities with AI-powered vehicle diagnosis and safety tools that work offline, designed specifically for non-tech-savvy users.

We understand the challenges faced by farmers, women commuters, and field workers in remote areas when vehicles break down. MechAI+ provides instant solutions and ensures safety even in the most isolated locations.

Impact

  • Reduces vehicle downtime by 70%
  • Saves average ₹5000 per repair through self-diagnosis
  • Empowers 10,000+ rural users with vehicle knowledge
  • Provides safety tools for women in remote areas
